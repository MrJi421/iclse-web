About the Conference

The Centre for Life Skills Education (CLSE) announces the International Conference on Life Skill Education, scheduled for March 11th & 12th, 2025. The conference is centered on the theme, ‚ÄúPeace & Harmony: Life Skills for a Better World‚Äù, and aims to explore the transformative power of life skills in fostering a harmonious and resilient global community. This first edition of the conference will provide a unique platform for sharing the latest insights, innovative approaches, and research trends in life skills education, as well as addressing current challenges and unexplored areas in the field.

The conference aspires to gather esteemed Academicians, Educators, Researchers, Practitioners, and Policy Makers from around the world to exchange ideas and present breakthroughs in life skills training and application. By fostering this exchange of knowledge, the event seeks to bridge the gap between academic research and the real-world implementation of life skills education. The conference will explore the role of life skills in promoting peace, enhancing interpersonal relations, improving emotional resilience, and addressing broader societal and environmental concerns.

Through collaborative dialogue and knowledge sharing, the International Conference on Life Skill Education is dedicated to advancing the essential role of life skills in building a peaceful and empowered global society.
