About Kristu Jayanti College

Kristu Jayanti College, founded in 1999, is managed by the members of St. Joseph Province of the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI). The College is affiliated to Bengaluru North University and is reaccredited with grade A++ in 2021 by NAAC in the third cycle of accreditation. The college is recognized by UGC under the category 2(f) & 12{B}. The College has been accorded Autonomous Status since 2013 by the University Grants Commission, The Government of Karnataka & Bangalore University. In the NIRF 2023 rankings, The college was placed among the top 150 colleges in the country and ranks as one of the five colleges from Karnataka. The programmes of School of Management are internationally accredited by the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs [ACBSP, USA]. The college was accorded ‘DBT Star College status under the strengthening component’ by the Department of Biotechnology, the Ministry of Science & Technology and the Government of India. The institution strives to fulfill its mission to provide educational opportunities for all aspiring young people to excel in life by developing academic excellence, fostering values, creating civic responsibility, inculcating environmental concern and building global competencies in a dynamic environment.

About Center for Life Skills Education

The Centre for Life Skills Education (CLSE) at Kristu Jayanti College, inspired by the World Health Organization's (WHO) recommendations, has been a pivotal force in advancing life skills education to foster positive and adaptive behavior among students, professionals, and community members. Established as Karnataka's Nodal Centre for Life Skills Education in 2012 in collaboration with the Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD), CLSE operates with the mission to build essential competencies in self-awareness, empathy, critical thinking, problemsolving, and stress management, among others. CLSE’s training programs reach undergraduate and postgraduate students across all disciplines, equipping them with skills for personal and professional resilience in today’s rapidly changing world. Extending its impact, CLSE also conducts targeted programs for government schools, corporate professionals, development workers, and healthcare providers, addressing specific needs such as stress management, gender sensitivity, and interpersonal skills.

Over the years, CLSE has achieved milestones in organizing capacity-building workshops, training of trainers (ToT) programs, and conducting extensive research to enrich life skills education methodologies. Notably, CLSE has also published studies and findings that guide new applications for life skills across various sectors. The center continues to serve as a model of excellence in life skills education, empowering individuals to face challenges with confidence and enhance their quality of life.
